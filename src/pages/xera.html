The Project

XERA is a unique and fully integrated cryptocurrency trading exchange that resolves the key issues and challenges that traders are facing with current exchanges today.

Some of XERA's features include High-performance trading (HPT), increase security, low fees, auto trading and fiat integration.

In November 2018, the project launched an ICO (Initial Coin offering). As a core member of the design team, I was responsible for exploring and developing UI/UX design soluions and prototypes for the exchange dashboard and platform prior to ICO launch.
